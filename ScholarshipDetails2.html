About The Scholarship

1.0 INTRODUCTION

Applications are invited from candidates currently pursuing their undergraduate studies at Public Universities (UA), Premier Polytechnics, and Government-Linked Universities (GLU) as identified by the Public Service Department (JPA) under the Convertible Loan (PBU) scheme.

The loan agreement is enforced under four PBU conditions as follows:

(i) The loan is converted into a full scholarship, exempt from repayment if the student serves in the Public Service upon completion of studies;
(ii) Repayment of 25% of the loan amount received if the student serves with a Government-Linked Company (GLC) after completing studies;
(iii) Repayment of 50% of the loan amount received if the student serves with a private company within the country after completing studies; and
(iv) In addition to the above conditions, students are required to fully repay the loan amount.

2.0 APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS

2.1 General Requirements:

1) Malaysian citizen;
2) Good health condition;
3) Parents not blacklisted by JPA or facing legal action by JPA;
4) Not receiving financial aid/sponsorship from any party at the same study level;
5) Possess Malaysian Certificate of Education (SPM); and
6) Age not exceeding 22 years in the application year (23 years for transfer students).

2.2 Specific Requirements:

1) Have commenced undergraduate studies before the closing date of the advertisement;
2) Meet academic criteria as follows:

(i) Students who have started their studies and achieved a Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) of at least 3.30; and
(ii) Have 12 months of remaining study excluding the current/ongoing semester.

2.3. Programs open for application are based on the list submitted by each participating institution and meeting the criteria set by JPA. The fields applied for by students should have full accreditation from the Malaysian Qualifications Agency (MQA) or recognition from Professional Bodies (if applicable).

2.4 Fields not sponsored include:

2.4.1 Medical Field
2.4.2 Dentistry Field
2.4.3 Pharmacy Field
2.4.4 Education Field
2.4.5 Fields requiring students to obtain specific licenses such as aviation licenses, diving licenses, etc.;
2.4.6 Programs in the form of public university franchises / IPTS
