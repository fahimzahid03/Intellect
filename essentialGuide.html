Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: What are the available study options after high school?

    A: After high school, you can explore various options such as attending a university, enrolling in a community college, pursuing vocational training, or entering the workforce.

  • Q: How do I choose the right college or university?

    A: Consider factors like program offerings, location, campus facilities, and accreditation. Research each institution to find the one that aligns with your academic and personal goals.

  • Q: What financial aid options are available for students?

    A: Students can explore scholarships, grants, student loans, and work-study programs. It's essential to apply for financial aid early and research all available options.
