About The Scholarship

1.0 ABOUT

Since 1975, Petronas has awarded education sponsorships to more than 36,000 deserving students of high academic calibre who dare to push boundaries with a performance mindset and leadership capabilities. This continues to be pursued by PETRONAS, which focuses on nurturing the right talent that will ultimately contribute to the growth of the company, industry and nation.

WHAT DOES THE SCHOLARSHIP OFFER?

(i) Up to RM10,000 a year in course and other fees including hostel fees, or the actual cost, whichever is lower;
(ii) Up to RM2,000 in allowances for learning aids including books, computer, stationeries and other materials per semester or up to RM4,000 per year;
(iii) Up to a maximum of RM6,000 allowance per year or RM500 per month;
(iv) There will be no bond to this scholarship and the recipients are not required to repay the amount of scholarship awarded.

2.0 APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS

The scholarship will be offered on a yearly basis to successful applicants, to undertake up to four (4) years of undergraduate studies locally. This notice of offer for the scholarship is open for applications from all Malaysian students in need of funding. Students who have started their undergraduate studies are also eligible to apply provided they meet the requirements.

1).You must be a Malaysian citizen under 25 years old;
2).You must have good academic results in SPM/STPM or equivalent (with credits in Bahasa Malaysia and English);
3). must be a full-time student and have gained admission into any of the courses related to the field of studies listed here, which has been approved and accredited by the Malaysian Qualifications Agency (MQA), in a college or university in Malaysia as approved by Cagamas;
4). Not be in a position to take on the course without financial aid or award;
5). Not be in receipt of any other scholarship, financial aid or award from any institution, organisation or foundation;
